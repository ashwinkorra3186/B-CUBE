Tata Chemicals

Social Enterprise Branding

Helped Tata Chemicals create a social enterprise-Brand, defining the soul and stage the brand to truly stand-out.

Challenge

Tata Chemicals needed to establish a distinctive social enterprise brand that would resonate with stakeholders while maintaining alignment with their corporate values.

Our Approach

  • Conducted comprehensive brand discovery to uncover the core essence of the social enterprise
  • Developed a differentiation strategy based on insights from stakeholder research
  • Created a brand positioning that highlighted the unique social impact
  • Crafted compelling brand messaging and communication framework

Results

The new social enterprise brand achieved increased market recognition and stakeholder engagement, establishing a strong foundation for sustainable growth and impact.

Market Intelligence & Entry Strategies

Challenge

TCS Lifesciences needed comprehensive market intelligence to inform their European expansion strategy and establish a strong market position in a competitive landscape.

  • Created brand equity measurement framework and communication strategy

PetKonnect

Brand Planning & International Launch

From feasibility to business planning and brand development to bring together pet parents to collaborate.

Challenge

PetKonnect needed to transform a passion-driven concept into a viable business with a strong brand identity that would resonate with pet parents globally.

Our Approach

  • Conducted feasibility analysis and developed comprehensive business plan
  • Assisted in discovery of the soul of the venture
  • Designed brand positioning based on the core purpose of the business
  • Created strategic brand planning for international launch
  • Developed engagement strategies for key stakeholders

Results

PetKonnect successfully launched with a clear brand identity and positioning, achieving strong market entry and brand recognition among pet parents.
